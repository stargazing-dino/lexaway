Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Lexaway is a free, open-source language learning app. Your privacy is simple to explain because we don't collect anything.

Data Collection

Lexaway does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no crash reporting, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

Internet Access

Lexaway may connect to the internet solely to download language packs. No personal information is sent during this process.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date at the top.
